What is the Hollow Bloom?

A mysterious phenomenon that appeared simultaneously across three locations: deep in the Amazon rainforest, beneath a war-torn megacity, and orbiting a distant exoplanet. Scientists call it an anomaly. Survivors call it a curse. But those who've touched it... they call it salvation.

Chapter 1: Origins

Karan discovers the first Hollow Bloom deep in the jungle, where ancient civilizations left warnings carved in stone. Survival becomes secondary to understanding.

Chapter 2: Convergence

Oedn uncovers corporate conspiracies in the city, where factions war over access to the Bloom's power. Trust no one. Question everything.

Chapter 3: Ascension

Aelly ventures to the stars, discovering that the Bloom connects worlds across dimensions. Humanity's fate hangs in the balance.
